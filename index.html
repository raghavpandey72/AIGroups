Valuation/Inspection of the property

Our company provides comprehensive valuation and inspection services to accurately assess the true worth and condition of various properties. Leveraging over 30 years of expertise, our Managing Director, Anand Behari Sharma, leads a team of skilled professionals in conducting meticulous inspections. We evaluate the structural integrity, quality of construction, and overall condition of residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Utilizing current market data, we determine the fair market value and identify potential risks and liabilities. Our detailed reports, including photographs and expert opinions, meet all insurance and legal requirements, ensuring clients receive accurate and reliable valuations. Clients benefit from our extensive industry knowledge and personalized advice, providing them with peace of mind in their property investments.

General Insurance Services

Our company specializes in a diverse range of insurance claims, including motor accident claims for OD, TPBI, and TPPD. We handle engineering claims, burglary claims, and business interruption claims like LOP and MLOP, ensuring clients are covered for unforeseen disruptions. Our services also include managing claims for deterioration of stock, shopkeeper's packages, and domestic insurance needs. We offer comprehensive coverage for workmen compensation, LPG multiperil policies, and personal accident claims. Additionally, we address mediclaims, money-in-transit, fidelity guarantee insurance, and marine claims. We also manage goods-in-transit claims, floricultural and greenhouse claims, and agriculture-related claims for crops, cattle, fisheries, and poultry. Our expertise ensures thorough and efficient handling of all types of insurance claims.

Liability Insurance Claims

Our company expertly handles a range of liability insurance claims, including compensation for third-party motor accident claims, both on a fault and no-fault basis. We focus on limiting liability against wrongdoers and addressing common legal liabilities. Our services ensure proper indemnification in cases of death, bodily injury, and third-party property damage. Additionally, we manage workmen's compensation claims (W.C) with meticulous attention, providing clients with comprehensive coverage and support throughout the claims process.

Life Insurance Claims

Our company offers expert handling of life insurance claims, ensuring that beneficiaries receive the support and compensation they are entitled to during challenging times. With over three decades of experience, our team provides thorough and compassionate assistance in processing claims, from verifying policy details to assessing the validity of claims. We manage all aspects of the claims process, including the verification of documentation and coordination with insurers to expedite payouts. Our goal is to provide clear, timely, and accurate support, ensuring that clients and their families receive the necessary financial relief and peace of mind during the claims process.

Identification and Handwriting

Our company specializes in advanced identification and handwriting analysis to uncover the authenticity of various documents. We address model forgery, traced forgery, and forgery by impersonation with precise forensic techniques. Our experts are skilled in detecting block forgery and identifying typewriting and stamped impressions. We also conduct thorough examinations of embossing paper seals and water seals to validate their integrity. Additionally, our services include the analysis of indented and disguised writing to reveal concealed or altered information. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and extensive expertise, we ensure accurate and reliable results for all forensic handwriting and document identification needs.

×

Fingerprints

Our company provides comprehensive fingerprint analysis services, encompassing a wide range of print types including latent fingerprints, chance prints, inked prints, block prints, palm prints, foot prints, and shoe prints. We utilize advanced techniques such as poroscopy for detailed analysis. Our experts are adept at lifting prints from crime scenes and understanding the correct methods for obtaining and preserving them. We employ scale photography to ensure accurate documentation of print evidence. By integrating these specialized techniques, we deliver precise and actionable forensic insights, crucial for solving cases and supporting legal proceedings.

×

Modus Operandi

Our company excels in analyzing the modus operandi of criminal activities through meticulous examination of crime scenes. We investigate the entrance and exit routes of culprits to understand their methods and movements during incidents. Our experts also analyze tool marks left at crime scenes, particularly in cases of theft and burglary, to identify the tools used and their potential impact. Additionally, we conduct thorough site inspections based on the principles of exchange, which examine how evidence is transferred between the perpetrator, victim, and crime scene. This comprehensive approach helps us reconstruct criminal activities accurately, providing crucial insights for investigations and legal proceedings.

Legal and General

Our company provides expert legal and general support services essential for insurance and forensic cases. We specialize in the recovery of insurance claims through subrogation, ensuring that rightful compensation is secured. Our professionals have extensive experience appearing in court as witnesses and cross-examiners, offering expert testimony in both insurance and forensic cases. We are skilled in taking detailed statements during investigations to support accurate case documentation. Additionally, we diligently follow up on the chain of fraud or crime, linking our findings with police investigations and prosecution efforts. This integrated approach ensures a comprehensive handling of legal and forensic matters, facilitating effective resolution and justice.
