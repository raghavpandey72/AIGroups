Identification and Handwriting

Our company specializes in advanced identification and handwriting analysis to uncover the authenticity of various documents. We address model forgery, traced forgery, and forgery by impersonation with precise forensic techniques. Our experts are skilled in detecting block forgery and identifying typewriting and stamped impressions. We also conduct thorough examinations of embossing paper seals and water seals to validate their integrity. Additionally, our services include the analysis of indented and disguised writing to reveal concealed or altered information. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and extensive expertise, we ensure accurate and reliable results for all forensic handwriting and document identification needs.

×

Fingerprints

Our company provides comprehensive fingerprint analysis services, encompassing a wide range of print types including latent fingerprints, chance prints, inked prints, block prints, palm prints, foot prints, and shoe prints. We utilize advanced techniques such as poroscopy for detailed analysis. Our experts are adept at lifting prints from crime scenes and understanding the correct methods for obtaining and preserving them. We employ scale photography to ensure accurate documentation of print evidence. By integrating these specialized techniques, we deliver precise and actionable forensic insights, crucial for solving cases and supporting legal proceedings.

×

Modus Operandi

Our company excels in analyzing the modus operandi of criminal activities through meticulous examination of crime scenes. We investigate the entrance and exit routes of culprits to understand their methods and movements during incidents. Our experts also analyze tool marks left at crime scenes, particularly in cases of theft and burglary, to identify the tools used and their potential impact. Additionally, we conduct thorough site inspections based on the principles of exchange, which examine how evidence is transferred between the perpetrator, victim, and crime scene. This comprehensive approach helps us reconstruct criminal activities accurately, providing crucial insights for investigations and legal proceedings.

Legal and General

Our company provides expert legal and general support services essential for insurance and forensic cases. We specialize in the recovery of insurance claims through subrogation, ensuring that rightful compensation is secured. Our professionals have extensive experience appearing in court as witnesses and cross-examiners, offering expert testimony in both insurance and forensic cases. We are skilled in taking detailed statements during investigations to support accurate case documentation. Additionally, we diligently follow up on the chain of fraud or crime, linking our findings with police investigations and prosecution efforts. This integrated approach ensures a comprehensive handling of legal and forensic matters, facilitating effective resolution and justice.
